The Process

Research

Research is the starting point for my design process as it's very important to understand your market and its trends. A good understanding supports a modern, dynamic, and timeless design that aids in your identity. This is where I also start to collect imagery for potential creative directions.

Mood Boards

Mood Boards are the best way to communicate visually. I use images collected through research to pull in colours, patterns, textures, and photos that visually represent the tone and theme of what you are looking for.

Paper Sketches

Starting designs on a computer can be limiting. I like to take the visual themes from your mood boards into the real world, this allows me to create expressive fonts and icons that lead to professional and purposeful designs.

Digital Concepts

Once I have a few strong alternative design concepts, I translate them graphically and create multiple distinct options.

Presentation

Presenting the digital concepts is an opportunity for us to discuss the design options and narrow them down to one. Final tweaks and adjustments will be made and then sent to you as a final rendering.

Brand Style Guide

Finally, I will provide a comprehensive Brand Style Guide that includes signature patterns, type combinations and various file types that complete your brand. This guide can be used for future references when creating marketing material and professional items.
